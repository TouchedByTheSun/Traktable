0.5

The 0.5 release of Traktable is a huge step forward in the development of the app. This is the first beta and includes a lot of new features, enhancements and bug fixes. The most interesting are listed below.

  • GUI: This is just a start. For now your movie and show history are shown
  • Errors made visible. Handy when shows won't show up on Trakt.tv and you don't understand why
  • More friendly first install experience
  • Scrobble queue for offline use or Trakt.tv downtime
  • Lot's of code fixes and enhancements
  • Translations to Dutch
